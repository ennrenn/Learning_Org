Leadership Development Specialists

Governance Structure

The councils and committees that oversee and guide L&D initiatives

L&D Council

Chair: Chief Learning Officer
Members: Representatives from all major business units
Frequency: Quarterly
Responsibilities: Reviews L&D strategy and priorities, approves major new initiatives

Skills Advisory Boards

Chair: Respective Functional Leaders
Members: Subject matter experts from the business
Frequency: Bi-monthly
Responsibilities: Provides input on specific skill needs and priorities, reviews curriculum for technical accuracy

Sustainability Skills Council

Co-Chairs: CSO and Senior L&D Leader
Members: Sustainability experts and business unit representatives
Frequency: Monthly
Responsibilities: Identifies sustainability competencies needed across roles, reviews sustainability content in learning programmes

Diversity & Inclusion Learning Council

Co-Chairs: CDO and Senior L&D Leader
Members: Diverse representatives from across the organisation
Frequency: Monthly
Responsibilities: Reviews learning content for inclusivity and representation, advises on DEI learning objectives and approaches

Experience Design Learning Council

Co-Chairs: CXO and Senior L&D Leader
Members: Customer experience, employee experience, and service design experts
Frequency: Monthly
Responsibilities: Reviews experience design learning content and approaches, identifies experience capability needs across the organisation

Learning Experience Committee

Chair: Rotating (employee volunteers)
Members: Employees from various levels and departments
Frequency: Monthly
Responsibilities: Provides feedback on learner experience, tests new learning approaches, acts as learning champions across the organisation
